📱 How to get iPhone reminders to ring
Notifications require the app to be installed:
1️⃣ Open in Safari (not Chrome)
2️⃣ Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
3️⃣ Open Dayrift from your Home Screen
4️⃣ Tap the toggle below → tap Allow
5️⃣ iOS Settings → Notifications → Dayrift → Allow

⚠️ Note: Reminders ring while the app is open. True background alerts require a server — not possible in a local app.
